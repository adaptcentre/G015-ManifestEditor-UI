Welcome to the website for the AHRC-funded Annotating the New Testament: Codex H, Euthalian Traditions, and the Humanities project, run by Prof Garrick Allen (University of Glasgow) and Dr Kimberley Fowler (University of Groningen), in partnership with the Early Manuscripts Electronic Library, the Manuscripts’ Digital Research and Study Laboratory, the Biblioteca nazionale universitaria di Torino, the Great Lavra monastery, and the Greek Orthodox Cathedral of St Luke, Glasgow. The project investigates the origins, transmission, and function of the Euthalian tradition, a series of lists, prefaces, cross-references, and historical texts, attached to Acts, Paul’s Letter, and the Catholic Epistles in hundreds of Greek New Testament manuscripts. You can find out more about the project, our team, publications, and digital edition of Codex H (GA 015), the earliest witness to the Euthalian tradition on this site.
